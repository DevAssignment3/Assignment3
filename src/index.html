Winter Break Schedule Announced

Posted: January 10, 2026

The university will observe winter break from January 15-31, 2026. Classes will resume on February 1, 2026. All administrative offices will remain closed during this period.

New Library Timing

Posted: January 8, 2026

Starting from January 15, 2026, the library will be open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends.

Student ID Card Renewal

Posted: January 5, 2026

All students are required to renew their ID cards before the start of the spring semester. Visit the administration office with your current ID and three passport-sized photographs along with birth certificates as well.
