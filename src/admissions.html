Admission Information

Spring 2026 Admissions Now Open

Application Deadline: February 15, 2026

The university is accepting applications for undergraduate and graduate programs for the Spring 2026 semester. Prospective students can apply online through the university admission portal. All required documents must be submitted before the deadline.

Programs Available: Computer Science, Business Administration, Engineering, Arts and Humanities, Natural Sciences

Undergraduate Programs

Start Date: March 1, 2026

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent with minimum 60% aggregate marks

Required Documents:

- Completed application form
- High school transcripts
- Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
- Two letters of recommendation
- Personal statement (500-750 words)
- Copy of ID/passport
- Application fee payment receipt

Application Fee: $50 (non-refundable)

Graduate Programs

Start Date: March 1, 2026

Eligibility: Bachelor's degree from an accredited institution with minimum CGPA of 3.0

Required Documents:

- Completed application form
- Official transcripts from all universities attended
- GRE/GMAT scores (program dependent)
- Three letters of recommendation
- Statement of purpose (1000-1500 words)
- Resume/CV
- Copy of ID/passport
- Application fee payment receipt

Application Fee: $75 (non-refundable)

International Student Admissions

Application Deadline: January 31, 2026

International students are encouraged to apply early as visa processing may take additional time. All international applicants must demonstrate English language proficiency through TOEFL (minimum 80 iBT) or IELTS (minimum 6.5 overall).

Additional Requirements:

- Proof of English language proficiency
- Financial documentation showing ability to cover tuition and living expenses
- Copy of passport
- Education credential evaluation (if applicable)

Scholarship Opportunities

Application Deadline: February 1, 2026

The university offers various scholarships for both domestic and international students based on academic merit and financial need.

Merit Scholarships: Up to 100% tuition waiver for exceptional students

Need-Based Aid: Financial assistance available for eligible students

Sports Scholarships: Available for outstanding athletes

Students must submit a separate scholarship application along with their admission application. All supporting documents must be provided by the deadline.

Transfer Student Admissions

Rolling Admissions

Students currently enrolled at other institutions may apply for transfer admission. Transfer credits will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Requirements:

- Minimum CGPA of 2.5 from current institution
- Official transcripts from all colleges/universities attended
- Transfer application form
- Statement explaining reason for transfer

A maximum of 60 credit hours may be transferred from previous institutions.

Campus Tour and Information Sessions

Every Saturday in January and February

Prospective students and their families are invited to attend campus tours and information sessions. Tours are conducted every Saturday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

Registration is required. Visit the admissions office website or call the admissions helpline to book your tour. Virtual tours are also available for those unable to visit in person.
